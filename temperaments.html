The Four Temperaments

Understand the classic behavioral patterns that shape our personalities.

SA

Sanguine

The Enthusiastic Extrovert

Sanguines are typically boisterous, bubbly, talkative, and extremely social. They are the life of the party and thrive on interactions with others. They are often optimistic and see the glass as half full.

Strengths

  • Highly social and outgoing
  • Creative and imaginative
  • Forgiving and doesn't hold grudges
  • Energetic and enthusiastic

Weaknesses

  • Can be disorganized or forgetful
  • Prone to over-committing
  • May struggle with follow-through
  • Highly sensitive to criticism

Communication Style

Expressive and animated. They use a lot of hand gestures and vocal variety. They love storytelling and may often go off on tangents.

CH

Choleric

The Driven Leader

Cholerics are goal-oriented, decisive, and ambitious individuals. They are natural leaders who enjoy taking charge and making things happen. They are motivated by results and efficiency.

Strengths

  • Decisive and confident
  • Practical and problem-solver
  • Highly disciplined and focused
  • Strong leadership abilities

Weaknesses

  • Can be impatient or irritable
  • May come across as domineering
  • Prone to workaholism
  • Struggles with showing vulnerability

Communication Style

Direct and concise. They focus on the "what" and "when" rather than the "how" or "why." They appreciate efficiency and getting straight to the point.

ME

Melancholic

The Analytical Perfectionist

Melancholics are thoughtful, analytical, and detail-oriented. They value quality, accuracy, and deep meaning. They are often introspective and have a high standard for themselves and others.

Strengths

  • Highly analytical and detail-oriented
  • Reliable and conscientious
  • Deeply thoughtful and reflective
  • Self-disciplined and persistent

Weaknesses

  • Prone to perfectionism and over-thinking
  • Can be moody or pessimistic
  • Often overly self-critical
  • May struggle with change

Communication Style

Precise and accurate. They value data and facts. They listen carefully and often take time to process information before responding.

PH

Phlegmatic

The Peaceful Diplomat

Phlegmatics are calm, steady, and easy-going. They value peace and harmony in their environment and relationships. They are excellent listeners and often act as mediators in conflicts.

Strengths

  • Calm and composed under pressure
  • Dependable and consistent
  • Great listening and mediation skills
  • Patient and understanding

Weaknesses

  • Can be passive or indecisive
  • May avoid conflict at all costs
  • Prone to procrastination
  • Can seem unenthusiastic or detached

Communication Style

Soft-spoken and diplomatic. They avoid confrontation and use inclusive language. They are attentive listeners who offer steady encouragement.
