The Four Temperaments

Most people are a blend, with one primary and one secondary influence.

Sanguine

Enthusiastic, Active, Social

Optimistic and expressive, often energized by interaction and variety.

Choleric

Driven, Decisive, Goal-Oriented

Results-focused and assertive, often stepping into leadership naturally.

Melancholic

Analytical, Careful, Reflective

Detail-oriented and thoughtful, with strong sensitivity to quality and depth.

Phlegmatic

Steady, Calm, Reliable

Consistent and diplomatic, often bringing stability and balance to groups.
